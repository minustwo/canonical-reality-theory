MST

MST is the structural canonicalization and admissibility layer of the CRT research stack.

It studies:

* admissible worlds
* interpretation spaces
* completion
* structural canonicality
* determinization
* Layer-1 limits
* future stratified core design

Public status

MST v2.16.4 is treated as an ARCHIVAL Layer-1 core.

It is not treated as a fully stratified final core.
