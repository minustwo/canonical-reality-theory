\begin{abstract}
This companion document contains full proofs for three groups of results
from \emph{When Correctness, Robustness, and Stability Coincide}.
Appendix~A covers the Bridge Hierarchy (Weak Bridge, Full Bridge, and
condition minimality). Appendix~B covers the Execution Layer theorems
(EL-1A, EL-1B-break, and the corrected EL-1B-ctrl split). Appendix~C
covers IET Endogenous Canonical Selection.

\textbf{v3 change}: Appendix~B updated to reflect the EL-1B correction
(2026-04-29). The original single-layer ctrl chain
$\Cmin^{ctrl,exec}\le\Cmin^{ctrl,proto}\le\Cmin^{ctrl,gov}$
has been constructively falsified by counterexample~X1. The correct
theorem split (EL-1B-break + EL-1B-ctrl-cumulative) replaces it.
\end{abstract}
