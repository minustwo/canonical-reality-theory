Example — conditional theorem artifact

Review status: Internally audited / AI-assisted review.

Example — audit artifact

Status: AUDIT-ONLY.
Peer review: Not externally peer reviewed.
Review status: Internal / adversarial audit.
AI assistance: AI-assisted audit may have contributed.
Citation status: Cite as audit record, not theorem.
Scope: Specific dependency, counterexample, or independence issue.
Dependencies: Relevant files or claims.
Known limitations: Does not establish theorem status by itself.

Example — superseded artifact

Status: SUPERSEDED.
Peer review: Not externally peer reviewed.
Review status: Historical artifact.
AI assistance: Unknown or recorded separately.
Citation status: Do not cite as current.
Scope: Historical version.
Dependencies: Previous version chain.
Known limitations: Replaced by newer artifact.
Replacement: Path or DOI of current version.
