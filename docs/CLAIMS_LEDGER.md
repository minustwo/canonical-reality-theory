Claims Ledger

Purpose

This ledger records the public claim-status landscape of the MST–CRT–IET research stack.

Status labels in this ledger follow `docs/STATUS_LABELS.md`.

Proof-supplement status is recorded in:
- `proofs/STATUS.md`
- `proofs/supplement/STATUS.md`
These ledgers are metadata/status files and do not modify theorem statements or proof content.

It is not a proof file.

It is not a theorem statement.

It is a status map.

Non-claims

This repository does not claim:

* full MST property (4) in general
* general AC-6 determinization for ICT without additional assumptions
* unconditional equivalence of correctness, robustness, and stability
* full stochastic stability of canonicality across all processes
* final proof of the complete MST–CRT–IET synthesis
* formal peer review unless explicitly stated

Citation discipline

Cite artifacts with their status.

Do not cite:

* CONDITIONAL artifacts as unconditional
* SKETCH artifacts as complete proofs
* AUDIT-ONLY artifacts as theorems
* SUPERSEDED artifacts as current
* Zenodo DOI records as peer-reviewed publications

Update rule

Any change to this ledger that modifies claim status must be treated as a research-status change, not metadata-only maintenance.
