# Claim Labels
## Purpose
This document standardizes claim labeling across the repository.
## Labels
### CONJECTURE
A proposed statement with no proof.
### SKETCH
A partially developed argument.
### PARTIAL
A result that holds under limited scope.
### CONDITIONAL
A result that depends on explicit assumptions.
### PROVED
A result with a complete and verified proof.
### AUDIT-LOCAL
A result verified only in internal or limited audit context.
### DEPRECATED
A result no longer considered valid or relevant.
## Rules
- Labels must not be omitted
- Labels must not be upgraded without justification
- Labels must be consistent with STATUS.md
## Forbidden
- Implicit promotion
- Silent relabeling
- Mixing labels within a single statement
