# Glossary
## Purpose
This glossary provides high-level terminology definitions for public navigation.
It does not replace formal definitions in technical papers.
## Terms
### Structure Theory (MST)
A framework for analyzing admissible interpretations under a set of constraints, rules, and inference policies.
### Determinization
The process of reducing plural admissible interpretations into a unique or selected interpretation under additional conditions.
### Canonical Reality (CRT)
A framework concerned with the stability and preservation of canonical interpretations under adversarial or structural pressure.
### Break Path
A conceptual representation of how a system transitions away from canonical structure.
### Escape Cost
A conceptual measure of how difficult it is to leave a canonical region.
### Institutional Layer (IET)
A layer that studies how real-world systems preserve or lose structural invariants.
## Warning
These are descriptive summaries, not formal definitions.
