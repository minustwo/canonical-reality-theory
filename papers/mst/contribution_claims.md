# MST Contribution Claims (Controlled)
## Purpose
This document defines how contributions may be stated without overclaim.
## Allowed claim types
### 1. Structural formulation
"We introduce a framework for analyzing admissible interpretation families."
---
### 2. Classification
"We distinguish between structural and epistemic plurality."
---
### 3. Problem formulation
"We formalize the determinization problem for structure theories."
---
### 4. Conditional results
"We provide conditions under which determinization may occur."
(Must remain conditional)
---
### 5. Limitations
"We identify cases where determinization fails."
---
## Disallowed claims
The following must NOT be stated unless formally proved:
- "We solve determinization in general"
- "We provide a complete theory"
- "We unify all related frameworks"
- "We establish equivalence with existing theories"
- "We guarantee uniqueness"
## Language discipline
Use:
- "may"
- "under conditions"
- "we study"
- "we analyze"
Avoid:
- "always"
- "complete"
- "fully general"
- "equivalent"
## Alignment rule
All claims must match:
- STATUS.md
- CLAIM_LABELS.md
## Final constraint
If uncertain, downgrade the claim.
